When passport control is successfully negotiated, proceed to the pickup area to collect the bags and then to the main entrance where our driver will be waiting just outside the main doors.
12:15pm - Malaga airport bus pickup
Once everyone is assembled, we go down to the bus in the parking area, the bags are loaded and we board the bus. The journey to the hotel is less than 15 minutes, where we unload the bags.
15:00pm - Hotel check-in
The rooms will not normally be available until 15:00 but we can sign in, get fitted with a "wrist-band" identifying us as "all-inclusive" and store the bags in a secure room. We are guests from then on.
