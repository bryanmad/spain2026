7:50am - flight arrives in Spain
After disembarking, passport control is phasing in a new procedure as from today, requiring UK nationals to register their biometric data ie.facial images and fingerprints. It may not be in place for us, but it is possible you may be asked to produce: Proof of Identity: usually your passport. Proof of Funds: confirmation you have sufficient funds for your stay. Return Ticket: proof of your return ticket. Accommodation Proof: hotel booking.
When passport control is successfully negotiated, proceed to the pickup area to collect the bags and then to the main entrance where our driver will be waiting just outside the main doors.
12:15pm - Malaga airport bus pickup
Once everyone is assembled, we go down to the bus in the parking area, the bags are loaded onto the trailer and we board the bus. The journey to the hotel is about 15 minutes, where we unload the bags.
15:00pm - Hotel check-in
The rooms will not normally be available until 15:00 but we can sign in, get fitted with a "wrist-band" identifying us as "all-inclusive" and store the bags in a secure room. We are guests from then on. There is a local par 3 course (a taxi ride away) at: - Miguel Angel Jimenez Golf Academy - C. Corelli, 2, 29620 Torremolinos, - Málaga, - Spain ... and local bars etc. a short walk away.
7:50am - Hotel rooms available
Rooms become available and we transfer the bags to the rooms etc. Meal times are: 08:00 - 10:30 Breakfast 13:00 - 15:00 Lunch 19:00 - 22:00 Dinner
